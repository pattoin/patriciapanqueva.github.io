Who I am

Human-centered, globally shaped

I'm a bilingual (English / Spanish) Senior UX Designer with 15+ years crafting multimodal and AI-enhanced experiences across voice assistants, open source, IoT, music technology, and consumer products.

Growing up in Colombia and living across 9 countries on 4 continents didn't just give me perspective — it gave me a lived understanding of how culture, language, and context shape what feels natural in a product. I design for the full spectrum of human diversity, not just a default user.

Being bilingual means I bring nuance to every culture I design for — and every team I work with.

Understand people first, design second. The process always starts with people — their context, motivations, and the moments that matter. That's what separates design that works from design that resonates.

How I Work

How I work

A very experienced designer doesn't just execute — they ask better questions. I'm always asking: how can this be clearer? Easier? More helpful for the person actually using it?

01
Deep research
Ethnographic studies, participatory design, behavioral insights. The real problem is rarely the obvious one.
02
Creative exploration
Concept ideation, storytelling, rapid visualization — from napkin sketch to working prototype.
03
Practical execution
Wireframes, multimodal prototypes, implementation specs. I bridge design vision and engineering reality.
04
Empathy first
I immerse in users' worlds — their frustrations, habits, and joys. Understanding people deeply IS the process.
05
Prototyping, iteration & AI
I prototype early and often — lo-fi to hi-fi, voice to multimodal — and use AI to accelerate each iteration cycle. Faster loops mean better decisions, earlier. The human perspective stays at the center, always.
06
Cross-functional leadership & AI agents
I align PM, engineering, research, and legal not just to ship — but to ship the right thing. Through experience narratives, working prototypes, and early stakeholder alignment, I make sure the UX vision doesn't get diluted between concept and code. Increasingly, I design for and with AI agents — shaping agentic workflows where humans and AI collaborate seamlessly.
07
Bridging physical & digital worlds
I'm passionate about the space where physical and digital meet — tangible objects that carry digital meaning, environments that respond to people, everyday products that feel natural because they respect the way humans actually live. The best experiences don't ask people to adapt to technology. Technology adapts to them.
08
Ethical technology design
Technology is only as good as the values it carries. I think deeply about the ethical dimensions of every product I work on — privacy, inclusivity, unintended consequences, and long-term impact on people's lives. Great design isn't just usable. It's responsible. It makes people's lives genuinely better, not just more efficient.
